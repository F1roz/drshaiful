Professional Summary

Precision, Compassion & Academic Leadership

Dr. Shaiful Islam is a dedicated and skilled Paediatric Surgeon with extensive experience in clinical practice, surgical procedures, and academic medicine. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at Bangladesh Medical University, he demonstrates a strong commitment to patient care, medical education, and professional development.

He possesses advanced training in neonatal surgery, minimally invasive surgery, paediatric urology, hepatobiliary surgery, and paediatric surgical oncology. He is recognised for academic leadership, surgical excellence, and lifelong dedication to community healthcare.

"Every child deserves surgical care that is precise, compassionate, and rooted in the best available evidence."
